🧠 Superstore Business Analysis (Excel)


📌 Problem
Retail businesses often struggle to understand which products drive profit and how discount strategies impact overall performance.


⚙️ What This Project Does
- Cleans and structures raw sales data
- Analyzes profitability across categories and sub-categories
- Evaluates the impact of discounts on profit
- Identifies high-performing regions and customer segments


📊 Key Insights

- **Technology** is the most profitable category (~145K profit)
- **Copiers** generate the highest profit (~55K), indicating strong margins
- **Tables** produce significant losses (~-17K), largely due to high discounting
- Profit declines sharply as discounts increase beyond 20%
- **West region** leads in profitability (~108K), while Central underperforms
- **Consumer segment** contributes the highest share of profit (~134K)


💡 Business Recommendations

- Maintain discounts at ≤20% to protect margins
- Restrict high discounts (>30%) to clearance scenarios
- Reassess pricing and cost strategy for loss-making products (e.g. Tables)
- Focus inventory and marketing efforts on high-performing categories like Technology
- Increase investment in high-performing regions (West)
- Develop targeted strategies to grow Corporate segment performance


🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Data Cleaning, Analysis)
